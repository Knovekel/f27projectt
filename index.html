Popular Destination

Maldives

The Maldives is world-famous for its myriad of marine life, and is considered to be one of the best snorkelling and diving locations in the world. Swim freely in the Indian Ocean among the colourful arrays of corals, rays, sea turtles, reef sharks and a multitude of other underwater species!.

Sydney

Sydney is home to must-visit icons like the Sydney Harbour Bridge and Opera House, but this Harbour City is constantly evolving. New rooftop bars, theatre shows and designer shops pop up at every turn, and the urban excitement is perfectly balanced by afternoons spent lying on the sand.

Tokyo

Tokyoites, known for politeness and discipline, embrace a harmonious blend of tradition and modernity. Experience the grace of a tea ceremony, witness the precision of a traditional martial arts performance, or participate in a festival celebrating centuries-old customs.

Manali

The hill station is a utopia if you want to experience majestic snow-clad mountains, spend some time in aloofness, and participate in adventure experiences. Located at 6260 feet above sea level, Manali always tops every tourist's bucket list. Continue reading to explore why you should choose Manali for your next trip

Paris

It is known as the most romantic city in the world, and is home to some world famous sights that are constantly shown in travel magazines, movies, and other works of art. Paris, the capital of France,has a population of over two million people and is one of Europe's most-visited cities.

Spain(Barcelona)

Overlooking the Mediterranean Sea, and famous for Gaudí and other Art Nouveau architecture, Barcelona is one of Europe's trendiest cities. It's a hub of new trends in the world of culture, fashion and cuisine. It combines the creativity of its artists and designers with respect and care for local traditions.
